Why Space Match

The idea for Space Match was born during a conversation with my co-founder Hitesh, while he was visiting me in the U.S. We started talking about India’s commercial real estate landscape — full of gatekeeping, guesswork, and outdated systems. It was clear:

This market doesn’t need another listing site. It needs infrastructure.

So we’re building exactly that — a high-trust platform powered by clean data, AI agents, and a product-first experience that helps users go from search to signed lease without the chaos.

It’s early, but we’re obsessed with the craft. And the conviction is real.
