The Space Match Story

Why We're Building This Now

The idea for Space Match began with a conversation during my co-founder Hitesh's visit to the U.S.

He had just gone through the frustrating process of leasing a commercial space in India — vague listings, outdated data, broker gatekeeping, and no central source of truth. We compared it to the U.S. — not perfect, but structured — and asked:

Why is India's $200B+ commercial real estate market still operating like it's 2005?

That moment sparked Space Match.

We're now building a platform that brings AI agents, clean data, and modern UX into Indian CRE — transforming the way space is discovered, compared, and leased. We're not just digitizing listings. We're rethinking the entire workflow with trust, speed, and user control at its core.

It's early — but we're building with long-term conviction.
